Agatha was a spectacular horticulturalist. She was better even than Sam who owned the flower shop. If there was something that Sam didn’t know or wasn’t sure about it was Agatha that she would go to. The two had worked together on multiple projects in the past. Recently a new flower shop had opened up in the next town over, it proprietor was none other than Sam’s old rival Rachel who wasn’t as knowledgeable as Sam when it came to Flowers.
