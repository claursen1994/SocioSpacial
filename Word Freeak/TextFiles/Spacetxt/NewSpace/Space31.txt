the party hall had been rented out for the high school’s prom. the theme was 1950’s and the hall had been decorated accordingly with bunting and posters from the era. to the right of the table with snacks and refreshments stood a photo booth, where students could have their pictures taken in their fancy dress consumes. to the right of the photo booth stood a table where students could write their well wishes to their fellow students, teachers, parents or anyone that had helped them get through the year.  this was set to be the biggest event in the school calendar.
